Welcome to Chicago's Parking App

Chicago's Parking App allows you to see which parking garages have availability and if crime has occurred in the area.

The app shows the availability of spaces at each specific location. Each garage will indicate if the parking spots are "available", "limited", or "unavailable". If status is "limited", we will show you the number of spaces that are left at this location. We will also show the price of parking for the available and limited parking spots.

We will display if any and what crimes occurred nearest the parking garage.
